Introduction :

A cryptocurrency wallet is a platform that allows users to earn, track, send and receive digital currencies. Unlike traditional banks or other pocket wallets those store currencies, cryptocurrencies are getting stockpiled as a transaction on Blockchain. So when a crypto exchange wallet receives a cryptocurrency, it means that the authority (ownership) of the cryptocurrency has been signed off to the wallet’s address.
